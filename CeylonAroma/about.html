Who We Are

Preserving the Authentic Legacy of Ceylon

Ceylon Aroma was founded with a clear, uncompromising vision: to restore Ceylon spices to their historical place of honor in the global luxury market. Our journey began in the southern hills of Matara, Sri Lanka, where our family-owned estates have grown and harvested Ceylon Cinnamon for over three decades.

Unlike mass-manufactured spices, Ceylon Aroma practices the artisanal peeling method. Our skilled cinnamon peelers (known locally as 'Kurundu Karayo') extract the inner bark in paper-thin layers, hand-rolling them into delicate quills that preserve the essential oils and subtle sweet aroma.

Today, we work with a cooperative network of over 500 smallholder farmers, ensuring that every clove, cardamom seed, and peppercorn carrying the Ceylon Aroma seal represents ethical sourcing and ultimate sensory purity.

Our Vision

To be globally recognized as the pinnacle of luxury spice exports, championing authentic Sri Lankan heritage, empowering local farming communities, and pioneering zero-waste, regenerative agriculture across our operations.

Our Mission

To source, sort, and deliver the highest quality, organic Sri Lankan spices to global culinary connoisseurs, wellness brands, and premium kitchens, while cultivating fair-trade partnerships that sustain the lives of our hard-working farming partners.

How We Craft Perfection

The Ceylon Aroma Process

01

Sustainable Harvesting

Our cultivators selectively harvest cinnamon shoots and spice pods only during optimal moisture seasons, preserving the roots for natural regrowth.

02

Artisanal Peeling

Experienced peelers hand-scrape the outer bark and peel the delicate, paper-thin inner layers, ensuring zero structural damage to the quills.

03

Natural Sun Drying

The spices are slowly dried in state-of-the-art solar dry domes, preventing dust exposure while capturing essential oil profiles.

04

Rigorous Grading

We grade our spices using international standards. Cinnamon is carefully categorized into Alba, Continental, and Hamburg grades based on diameter.

05

Organic Fumigation

We utilize ozone sterilization and gentle temperature treatments for pest control, completely avoiding harmful chemical gases.

06

Eco-Friendly Packaging

Our spices are vacuum-packed in biodegradable foil bags and shipped in luxury, recycled timber chests to maintain aroma freshness.

Exporting the finest organic spices from Sri Lanka. Deeply committed to preserving our natural environment and maintaining the traditional farming heritage of our local communities.
